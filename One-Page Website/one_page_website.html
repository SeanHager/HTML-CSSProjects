Welcome to Lunita's Lunchbox! Where lunch is the best time of the day!

So who is Lunita? Click me to find out!

"Lunita" or "Lulu" are nicknames for my daughter Luna. She is our miracle baby after suffering many preganacy losses. This food truck project is dedicated to her!

All of our food specials are named after our family, who we cherish the most. Click our items for more information!
